COOPY—大学生心理健康应用程序

关于项目

在探讨健康主题时，我选择了ACM CHI 2021年的论文集，从中找到问题并决定以“为情绪健康而设计”作为主题。经过阅读相关论文，并了解自我效能感、学习压力和学习倦怠等方面，我选定了学习倦怠作为主要问题。最终，基于ICBT技术和相关理论，我试图将劝导式方法和定制化技术融入心理健康工具的设计中，为大学生提供可用的数字心理健康工具。

时间：2022年秋季（2022年10月）

背景：在校课程

团队：独立完成

职责：用户研究、UX设计、原型设计、可用性测试、界面设计等

工具：Figma、AI

问题陈述

我们如何帮助大学生解决学习倦怠问题？

解决方案

我们提出了一种应用程序，它为职业倦怠患者提供人工智能辅助的恢复治疗，由持有执照的咨询师在规定的时间内介入。此外，该应用程序还提供支持小组，以促进用户与经历过倦怠恢复的人互动。 该应用程序将采用哥本哈根倦怠问卷（CBI）来测量用户的倦怠水平，并根据用户的回答，人工智能将为用户设计一个定制的路线图。这个定制的路线图是基于互联网的认知行为疗法（ICBT）技术/步骤的一套方案，这是一种广泛使用的、经过临床验证的减少倦怠的方法。 根据从问卷中计算出的倦怠程度，人工智能将决定治疗师介入的频率。用户将拥有一个仪表板，用于跟踪和管理他们在康复过程中的进展，以及有关治疗师会议的信息。 此外，该应用程序还将为用户提供一份活动清单，用户可以通过这些活动来放松自己，或者了解其他策略来帮助他们在紧急情况下应对。通过包括支持小组，用户可以获得社会和道德支持，从而提高在线治疗的效果。用户可以随时访问这些经过调整的支持小组。人工智能系统将通过识别小组中的关键词来调整支持小组。

设计背景

倦怠（burnout）一直是心理健康领域的热点话题。心理学中有关倦怠的研究最初起始于工作领域，如今已扩展和细化到学习领域，主要涉及学习倦怠的研究。学习倦怠是一种主要发生在学生身上持续的、负性的、与学习相关的心理状态，这种状态主要表现为身心耗竭、学业疏离和低成就感等方面。观察数据显示，截至2021年，寻求网上心理健康帮助的人数增加了500％，其中45％为18岁以下的年轻人。因此，探讨学习倦怠及其影响因素，并为学生们提供相应的心理健康服务，对维护学生的心身健康具有重要意义。

Users can customize their experience based on their goals, experience level, and time constraints. Headspace offers a free trial and paid subscription options starting at $12.99 per month. Customer support is available via email, and the app has a 4.9-star rating on the App Store.
BetterHelp:
BetterHelp is an online therapy platform that offers professional counseling services for various mental health issues. BetterHelp matches users with licensed therapists based on their needs and preferences. Users can communicate with their therapists through text, video, or voice messages. BetterHelp offers subscription-based pricing starting at $80 per week. The app has a 4.6-star rating on the App Store, and customer support is available via email.
Happify:
Happify is an app that uses evidence-based interventions to improve mental health and wellbeing. The app offers various activities and exercises based on cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), positive psychology, and mindfulness. Users can track their progress and set goals to work towards. Happify offers a free trial and subscription-based pricing starting at $14.99 per month. The app has a 4.8-star rating on the App Store, and customer support is available via email.
Mindshift:

The app offers relaxation exercises, mindfulness practices, and cognitive restructuring techniques. Users can customize their experience based on their goals and preferences. Mindshift is free to download and use, and customer support is available via email. The app has a 4.7-star rating on the App Store.
Reflectly:
Reflectly is an AI-powered journaling app that helps users to reflect on their emotions and daily experiences. The app uses machine learning algorithms to provide personalized insights and recommendations based on the user's input. Reflectly also offers guided meditations and mindfulness exercises. The app offers a free trial and subscription-based pricing starting at $3.99 per month. The app has a 4.6-star rating on the App Store, and customer support is available via email.

用户研究

我采访了六位不同年级不同专业背景的人，以更好地了解我们想要定位的用户群体，并理解用户的痛点和需求。

研究洞察

通过分析用户访谈，我得到了以下洞察点。
User pain points:
Experience symptoms of fatigue, stress, anxiety, insomnia, headache, and low productivity due to academic pressure.
Find it challenging to talk to close ones about their problems due to lack of confidentiality, fear of burdening them, and occupying their time.
Face the risk of being carried into the later stages of their career if academic burnout is left untreated or unmanaged.
Compromise their eating and sleeping schedules due to the pressure of completing tasks within strict deadlines, which can adversely impact their health.
Struggle to find therapists due to the high cost of the solution, which they cannot afford based on their economic background.
User needs:
A solution that can help them manage their academic pressure and prevent academic burnout.
A platform where they can communicate with people who have similar experiences and are willing to listen to their problems without judgment.
A tool that can monitor their health and provide suggestions to maintain a healthy lifestyle.
An affordable solution to manage mental health issues.

设计目标

制定了设计目标

设计策略

制定了设计策略。
Problem Analysis:

Lack of Mental Health Awareness:
Students are not aware of the importance of maintaining good mental health, and how it can impact their academic performance and overall well-being. The problem is further exacerbated by the lack of resources and support available to students for addressing their mental health concerns.

High levels of stress and anxiety:
Students are facing high levels of stress and anxiety due to academic pressure, financial constraints, and personal issues. This is leading to physical and emotional symptoms such as headaches, fatigue, insomnia, and decreased productivity.

Difficulty accessing mental health resources:
Students face challenges in accessing mental health resources due to a lack of availability, affordability, and confidentiality. There is also a stigma associated with seeking mental health support, which prevents many students from seeking the help they need.

Inadequate time management skills:
Many students struggle with managing their time effectively, which leads to increased stress and pressure. Poor time management can also result in compromising other aspects of their health, such as sleep and diet.

Lack of social support:
Students may not have a supportive social network to turn to when dealing with academic or personal stressors. This can lead to feelings of isolation and loneliness, which can further exacerbate mental health problems.

Potential Solutions:

Increase Mental Health Awareness:
Developing and implementing mental health awareness programs and initiatives can help students understand the importance of maintaining good mental health. These programs can include workshops, webinars, and seminars to educate students about the importance of mental health, how to recognize symptoms, and how to seek support.

Develop Time Management Skills:
Providing resources and training to help students manage their time effectively can alleviate stress and pressure. This can include providing tools and techniques to help students prioritize their workload and balance their time effectively.

Increase Accessibility to Mental Health Resources:
Developing a network of affordable and confidential mental health resources can help students access the support they need. This can include counseling services, mental health hotlines, and online resources.

Create a Supportive Environment:
Establishing a supportive environment can help students feel more comfortable seeking help for their mental health concerns. This can include creating peer support groups, developing mental health programs, and training staff to recognize and respond to student mental health needs.

Address Stigma and Barriers to Seeking Help:
Working to address the stigma associated with seeking mental health support can help reduce barriers to access. This can include creating campaigns to increase awareness, providing education about mental health, and promoting a culture of openness and support.

功能架构

根据设计策略，制定了功能架构。
Introduction:
The proposed application is designed to provide AI-assisted recovery therapy to individuals suffering from occupational burnout. It will be an application that will involve licensed counselors and will provide a customized roadmap for recovery. The AI technology will be used to provide a tailor-made roadmap based on the responses provided in the Copenhagen Burnout Inventory (CBI). The therapy will involve cognitive-behavioral techniques, which have been clinically validated for reducing burnout. The application will also provide support groups to encourage user interaction with people who have experienced burnout recovery.

Functional Requirements:
User Login and Signup: Users can create an account with the application using their email or social media credentials. Once logged in, users will be prompted to fill out the CBI questionnaire.

Copenhagen Burnout Inventory (CBI) Questionnaire: The application will use the CBI questionnaire to measure the user's level of burnout. The responses will be analyzed by the AI system to provide a tailored recovery plan.

AI-Assisted Therapy: Based on the results of the CBI questionnaire, the AI system will design a customized roadmap for the user. The roadmap will include cognitive-behavioral techniques that have been clinically validated for reducing burnout.

Dashboard: The user will have access to a dashboard where they can track their progress and access information about their therapy sessions.

Counselor Meetings: The user will have access to licensed counselors who will provide therapy sessions at scheduled intervals. The frequency of counselor meetings will depend on the level of burnout calculated from the CBI questionnaire.

Support Groups: The application will have support groups where users can interact with others who have experienced burnout recovery. The AI system will adjust the support groups based on the keywords identified in the user's responses.

Activity List: The application will provide a list of activities that users can perform to relax or to learn new strategies for coping in stressful situations.

Notifications: The application will provide notifications to remind users of scheduled therapy sessions and other important events.

Payment Gateway: The application will have a payment gateway to facilitate payments for the therapy sessions.

原型测试

绘制了低保真原型，并开展了简单的用户测试。

流程1：通过有同情心的引导了解用户。

问题：
· 从初步研究中，我们发现用户在与亲近的人交谈时遇到问题，认为向他们解释问题可能会浪费时间。 这有时会导致他们对自己的实际感受不诚实。
· 他们还想与专业人士交流，这最终会给他们留下他们没有被评判的印象。
· 我们还发现，与治疗师交谈会给他们一种被倾听的感觉，尽管他们很难将自己的想法传达给其他人。
解决方案：
· 语音辅助头像 — Clara：
我们设计了一种更加用户友好的入职体验，以帮助客户轻松开放并习惯于在康复路线图中与治疗师沟通的想法。除了为用户提供手动输入外，我们还想鼓励他们用语言表达他们的回答。
· CBI 问卷：
哥本哈根职业倦怠清单是问卷的名称。治疗师一直在使用这份问卷作为他们日常程序的一部分，以确定用户在治疗期间的倦怠程度。我们可以通过询问用户这些问题，然后相应地调整恢复路线图来确定用户的疲劳程度。
