Overview

Evangelical Lutheran Church Hong Kong (ELCHK) was faced with increasing demand for their home-care service due to the aging situation in Hong Kong. They needed to revamp service flow and offerings to increase workflow efficiency and service quality.I was responsible for the user research, design system development and UX/UI design for one of the three products, which increase internal workflow efficiency and facilitate collaboration among employees.

 

 

Background

With the development and popularization of the Internet, online learning communities have gradually increased, which has built a good communication platform for knowledge exchange and sharing among teachers and students in colleges and universities. The learning and living spaces of college students are characterized by dispersal, fewer class concepts, and changeable living and learning locations, which limit the knowledge exchange in real life. Internet for more professional and academic help.

 

 

Research

The research objects are college students in Guangzhou.Collect pain points and needs through online questionnaires.Ask questions around academic communication behaviors, Starting from user attitude and platform experience, ask questions about academic communication behavior.
