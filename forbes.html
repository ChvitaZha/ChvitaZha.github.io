UX design | 2021

圆桌—大学生知识交流与共享平台

Optimizing the reading experience of the forbes app. Focuses on people’s frustration and goals and come up with the solution that is easy to use and equitable.

Overview

As a 32 Weeks Intensive Design Course from Google UX Program, This UX study aims to understand forbes's users needs and frustration. This project has been done for educational purposes and is in no way made, owned, or affiliated directly with any application

My Role

Role: UX Researcher & UX Designer
Timeline: 10 Days
Context: Personal Project under the mentorship Google Mentors from Coursera.

Background

I started my research by reading user reviews on the app store. Many users rated the forbes app low rating mainly because of two reason, first is the design of the app is not good and the user experience of reading articles and news is bad.
